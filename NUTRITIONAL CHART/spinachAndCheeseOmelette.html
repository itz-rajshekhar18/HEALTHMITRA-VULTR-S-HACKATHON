Spinach And Cheese Omelette Recipe





Ingredients:

2 large eggs

1/2 cup spinach, chopped

1/4 cup grated cheese (cheddar or feta)

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tsp olive oil



Instructions:
  • Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan.
  • Saut√© the spinach until wilted.
  • In a bowl, beat the eggs, add cheese, salt, and pepper.
  • Pour the egg mixture over the spinach in the pan.
  • Cook until the omelette sets, flipping halfway.
  • Serve warm with whole-grain toast.
