Salmon And Veggie Foil Pockets Recipe





Ingredients:

2 salmon fillets

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cup zucchini, sliced

1 tbsp olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

1 lemon, sliced



Instructions:
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Place each salmon fillet in a large piece of foil.
  • Add tomatoes, zucchini, salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil on top.
  • Place lemon slices over salmon, then fold the foil into packets.
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes until salmon is cooked through.
  • Serve warm.
