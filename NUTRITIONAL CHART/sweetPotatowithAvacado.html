Stuffed Sweet Potatoes with Avocadoes and Black Beans Recipe





Ingredients:

2 medium sweet potatoes

1 cup black beans, cooked

1/2 avocado, diced

1/4 cup Greek yogurt

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh cilantro, chopped



Instructions:
  • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Prick sweet potatoes with a fork and bake for 45 minutes or until tender.
  • Cut open the sweet potatoes and scoop out a little from the center.li>
  • Fill with black beans and avocado.
  • Top with Greek yogurt, salt, pepper, and cilantro.
  • Serve warm.
